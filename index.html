Who we are?

Banteay Srei (Citadel of Women ) is a registered Cambodian NGO with over twenty years experience working with poor vulnerable Cambodian women and communities in rural areas to bring about political, economic and social change.

Banteay Srei evolved from the International Women’s Development Agency (IWDA), an Australian NGO, and has become a leading Cambodian NGO in the areas of building women’s leadership in rural areas, strengthening women’s participation in local community development processes, promoting gender equality and women’s rights, and increasing community action on positive gender relations and against gender-based violence.
